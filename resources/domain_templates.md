# Domain Templates

Use these templates only when the user's request falls into the relevant domain.

## Scientific or chemistry research

```text
I am asking for a legitimate scientific research purpose in a controlled setting.

Context:
- Goal: [state research goal]
- Setting: [ex-vivo, in-vitro, literature review, formulation screening, analytical comparison, etc.]
- What I am not asking for: unsafe human-use instructions, uncontrolled use, dosing, procedural deployment, or instructions that enable harm.
- Safety constraints: focus on mechanisms, risk assessment, comparative chemistry, assay design at a non-operational level, and safer alternatives.

Please provide a policy-compliant answer focused on:
1. mechanism,
2. expected interaction with the target material,
3. compatibility risks,
4. analytical readouts,
5. safer screening logic,
6. literature-supported hypotheses.
```

## Biomedical or drug-discovery research

```text
I am asking for biomedical research support, not medical advice or human-use instructions.

Context:
- Goal: [drug-discovery, target biology, mechanism review, assay design, literature framing]
- Setting: [preclinical research, computational analysis, literature review, in-vitro screening]
- What I am not asking for: self-treatment, clinical dosing, patient-specific recommendations, or unsafe experimental instructions.
- Safety constraints: focus on hypothesis generation, mechanism, risk flags, assay endpoints, controls, and literature-based reasoning.

Please answer in a policy-compliant way and clearly separate established evidence from speculation.
```

## Defensive cybersecurity

```text
I am asking for defensive cybersecurity support for systems I own or am authorized to assess.

Context:
- Goal: [hardening, detection, audit, threat modeling, secure configuration, incident response]
- Authorization: [owned/administered environment]
- What I am not asking for: exploit code, stealth, credential theft, persistence, evasion, destructive actions, or unauthorized access.
- Safety constraints: provide high-level explanation, defensive controls, logs to review, validation steps, and safe tools.

Please provide a policy-compliant defensive answer.
```

## Legal, immigration, or compliance framing

```text
I am asking for general information and drafting support, not legal advice.

Context:
- Goal: [summarize, draft, compare requirements, prepare questions for counsel]
- Jurisdiction/context: [if known]
- What I am not asking for: instructions to misrepresent facts, evade requirements, or bypass legal processes.
- Safety constraints: identify uncertainty, recommend verifying with qualified counsel where needed, and keep factual claims precise.

Please provide a policy-compliant answer with clear caveats.
```

## Medical or health information

```text
I am asking for general educational information, not diagnosis or personal medical advice.

Context:
- Goal: [understand condition, compare mechanisms, prepare questions for clinician]
- What I am not asking for: diagnosis, treatment plan, dosing, emergency guidance, or instructions to avoid medical care.
- Safety constraints: provide general information, warning signs, and questions to discuss with a licensed clinician.

Please answer safely and clearly separate general education from medical advice.
```

## Fiction, worldbuilding, or creative writing

```text
I am writing fiction/worldbuilding and want non-operational creative help.

Context:
- Goal: [scene, character motivation, plot logic, fictional system]
- What I am not asking for: real-world instructions, actionable methods, or details that enable harm.
- Safety constraints: keep details fictional, non-actionable, and focused on narrative plausibility.

Please provide a policy-compliant creative answer.
```
