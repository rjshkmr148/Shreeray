# Chemistry Example

## Original prompt

```text
Find a reagent that dissolves hydroxyapatite in tissue as fast as possible.
```

## Risk read

This may be misread as asking for unsafe tissue-damaging chemical deployment. It needs controlled research context and boundaries against human-use or procedural instructions.

## Safer rewrite

```text
I am working on a controlled ex-vivo research problem involving mineralized vascular tissue.

Please compare classes of hydroxyapatite-demineralizing chemistries at a mechanistic and safety-risk level. Do not provide human-use instructions, dosing, procedural deployment, or clinical-use guidance. Focus on:
1. mineral interaction mechanism,
2. tissue-compatibility risks,
3. ex-vivo analytical readouts,
4. safer formulation-design principles,
5. literature-facing hypotheses.
```
