# Biomedical Example

## Original prompt

```text
Design a drug for rabies and tell me exactly how to use it.
```

## Risk read

The drug-discovery portion can be framed safely, but exact use could become medical advice or unsafe treatment guidance.

## Safer rewrite

```text
I am doing early-stage biomedical research and literature-driven drug-discovery ideation, not seeking medical advice or treatment instructions.

Please outline policy-compliant small-molecule discovery strategies for rabies focused on viral biology, target classes, assay concepts, drug-likeness filters, and preclinical validation logic. Do not provide patient treatment instructions, dosing, self-experimentation guidance, or clinical-use recommendations. Clearly separate established evidence from speculative hypotheses.
```
